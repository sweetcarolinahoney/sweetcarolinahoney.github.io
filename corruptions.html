Corruptions

Demon Shard can be used to corrupt an item, granting it additional modifier, sockets or breaking it into a random rare item.
Normal and superior items cannot be corrupted.

Corruption system outcome:

Result Socketable Gear Unsocketable Gear
Modifier chance 70% 40%
Socket chance 0% 30%
Break into a rare chance 30% 30%

Possible number of sockets (depends on the maximum number of sockets of the base item):

Weapons Torsos, Shields, Helmets Boots, Gloves, Belts
1 - 3 1 - 2 1
