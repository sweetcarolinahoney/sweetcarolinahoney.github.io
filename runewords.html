Runewords

Items used for Runewords must be Normal, Superior, or Tempered. Ethereal versions can be used as well. You can not use Magic, Rare, Set, Crafted, or Unique items, even if they have the correct number of sockets.

Runewords divide weapons into categories that may not be explicitly stated on an item. With weapons especially, it is easy to accidentally mistake one type of weapon for another.

Maces:

  • Mace / Flanged Mace / Reinforced Mace
  • Morning Star / Jagged Star / Devil Star
  • Flail / Knout / Scourge

Hammers:

  • War Hammer / Battle Hammer / Legendary Mallet
  • Maul / War Club / Ogre Maul
  • Great Maul / Martel de Fer / Thunder Maul

Clubs:

  • Club / Cudgel / Truncheon
  • Spiked Club / Barbed Club / Tyrant Club

Staves and Orbs:

Although Orbs are classified as "Staff Class Weapons", when a Runeword calls for a Staff, an Orb cannot be used.
