Consumables

Consumables are the new type of items that can be dropped from monsters. The purpose of consumables is to make cube recipes easier to use. Now you don't need to transmute a lot of ingredients to make wishful item.

List of the consumables:

Blacksmith's Whetstone

  • Cube w/ weapon, armor, arrows or bolts to repair & recharge it
  • You cannot repair Ethereal items

Sharpened Chisel

  • Cube w/ armor or weapon to remove socketables from sockets
  • Any gems, runes, or jewels will be destroyed

Forged Tool

  • Cube w/ Normal, Superior, or Tempered item to add sockets
  • 5% chance the item become Ethereal

Lesser Malus

  • Cube w/ weapon or armor to upgrade it from Base to Exceptional
  • Normal and Superior items cannot be upgraded

Greater Malus

  • Cube w/ weapon or armor to upgrade it from Exceptional to Elite
  • Normal and Superior items cannot be upgraded

Magic Dice

  • Cube w/ Magic item to reroll stats on it

Rare Token

  • Cube w/ Rare item to reroll stats on it

Mystic Orb

  • Cube w/ Perfect Gem and Magic item to make Crafted item

Kurast Sphere

  • Cube w/ Unique or Set item to reroll stats on it
  • You'll get the item with different stats or another item of the same type
  • Rings, Amulets, Charms and Jewels cannot be rerolled

Enchanted Malus

  • Cube w/ Normal or Superior item to get Magic or Rare item
  • 2/3 chance to get Magic item, 1/3 - Rare item

Demon Shard

  • Cube w/ item to make it Corrupted
  • Normal and Superior items cannot be corrupted

Tempering Catalyst

  • Cube w/ Normal or Superior item to make it Tempered
  • Tempered items are used for making Runewords that can be Corrupted

Heroic Consumables

You can use unwanted items to get points, then spend it to make Heroic version of currency. Cube unwanted items with the Mark of the Devil and get points. The Mark of the Devil drops from Uber Diablo in Nightmare difficulty. More info can be found here.

List of items to get points:

  • Basic Unique or Set item: 3
  • Exceptional Unique or Set item: 5
  • Elite Unique or Set item: 10
  • Unique or Set Ring/Amulet: 5
  • Any Crafted item: 3
  • Flawless Gem: 1
  • Perfect Gem: 3
  • Rare Small, Large, Grand Charms: 3
  • Rare Jewel, Amulet, Ring: 1
  • Unique Jewel: 20
  • Hellfire Torch: 15
  • Annihilus: 10
  • Gheed's Fortune: 5
  • Io, Lum, Ko, Fal, Lem Runes: 1
  • Pul Rune: 3
  • Um Rune: 5
  • Mal Rune: 10
  • Ist Rune: 15
  • Gul Rune: 20
  • Vex Rune: 30
  • Ohm Rune: 50
  • Lo Rune: 75
  • Sur Rune: 100
  • Ber Rune: 125
  • Jah Rune: 150
  • Cham Rune: 200
  • Zod Rune: 250

When you collected enough points, cube currency with Mark of the Devil to create Heroic version of the currency listed below.

Heroic Blacksmith's Whetstone (cost: 100 points)

  • Cube w/ Ethereal weapon or armor to repair it

Heroic Sharpened Chisel (cost: 50 points)

  • Cube w/ armor or weapon to remove socketables from sockets
  • Any gems, runes, or jewels and the item itself will be restored

Heroic Forged Tool (cost: 100 points)

  • Cube w/ Magic, Rare, Crafted, Set, or Unique item to add sockets
  • Possible number of sockets is the same as in corruption recipes

Heroic Magic Dice (cost: 50 points)

  • Cube w/ Magic item and Flawless Gem to add a Gem Affix
  • Amulets, Rings, Jewels and Charms cannot be used

Heroic Rare Token (cost: 75 points)

  • Cube w/ Rare item and Flawless Gem to add a Gem Affix
  • Amulets, Rings, Jewels and Charms cannot be used

Heroic Kurast Sphere (cost: 100 points)

  • Cube w/ Unique Amulet, Ring, Rainbow Facet or Gheed's Fortune to reroll stats on it
  • You'll get the item with different stats or another item of the same type

Heroic Enchanted Malus (cost: 150 points)

  • Cube w/ Normal or Superior item to get Unique item

Heroic Demon Shard (cost: 200 points)

  • Cube w/ Standard of Heroes to add a modifier
